In the format X > Y / A __ B, X is the "input", Y is the "output", "A __ B" is the "context", A is the prior context, B is the posterior context. X, Y, A, and B are variables that could be involve one or more single segments or classes of segments, captured in feature matrices, e.g. [+syl].
